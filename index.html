German sheperd

German Shepherds are intelligent, loyal, and athletic herding dogs known for their confidence, courage, and versatility as working dogs (police, military, rescue),
and family companions, requiring significant physical and mental exercise

Golden retriever

Golden retriever's are often safety dogs, they are also very good for protection of their owner.They are a kind and loving breed, which is why they make good pets!

Shitzoo

Shih Tzus are affectionate, people-oriented lap dogs from Tibet/China, known for their long, silky hair, big eyes, and charming, sometimes stubborn!

Pomeranian

Pomeranian's are very cute and are mostly house pets, they are not used often for protection animals seeing as they are very small. They are very furry and are quite entertaining!

Husky

Huskies are energetic, intelligent sled dogs known for their thick double coats, striking eyes (often blue or heterochromatic), and wolf-like appearance, originating from Siberia.

Poodle

Poodles are exceptionally intelligent, active, and elegant dogs, famous for their curly, low-shedding coats, available in Standard, Miniature,
and Toy sizes, originally bred as water retrievers in Germany/France.
