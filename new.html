What is the most famous breeds?

The most famous and popular dog breeds currently are led by the German Sheperd, which holds the top spot for its adorable activity and affectionate, large-sized nature.
Following closely are the Labrador Retriever, Golden Retriever, French Bulldog, and Poodle. These breeds are known for their intelligence, loyalness, and,
in many cases, good for family life or apartment living.
